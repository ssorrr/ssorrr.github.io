About Me

I always enjoy being able to plan and create things. Whether it's dumb drawings, painting Warhammer models or building this website, I love making things that I can be proud of. I have the same level of enthusiasm when it comes to my career: when being given tasks or projects, I feel a great sense of pride and accomplishment planning things out, working through each step and ironing out any problems, and being able to show it off once it's finished.

I've worked with a wide range of programming languages, though my main professional experience is with web development languages like PHP, HTML and Javascript. I am quite familiar with Python, as it's the language I used to learn programming when I was in school. I also learnt Lua for my final project at A-Level, and I'm always looking for opportunities to learn new programming languages and broaden my skills.
